About Caritakathon

What is Caritakathon

Caritakathon, a portmanteau of caritas and hackathon, is a 2-day hackathon where teams of 3 design and build websites for charities and non-profit organizations. Prizes will be given to the team and charity whose website has the best execution of the client brief and fulfillment of the judges' criteria.

TimeLine

  • AUGUST 21 Formal Launch
  • SEPTEMBER 18 Pre-event Bootcamp
  • OCTOBER 5-6 Caritakathon

Mechanics

Consultation

Each team will have 2 hours to meet with their respective clients regarding the website needs, standards, design, etc.

After the meeting with their respective clients, teams will have 2 hours to discuss their strategy then the hackathon begins.

Presentation

Each team will have a maximum of 30 minutes to present to a panel of judges on the 2nd day of the hackathon, at 4:00 pm.

Mechanics

  1. Each team should be able to present a brochure type site with 5 minimum pages
  2. Websites should be responsive and mobile friendly.
  3. Use of existing CMS themes/templates are discouraged.
  4. Responsiveness, mobile first concepts & usability + accessibility are central goals.

Criteria

  • Aesthetics (45%)
    • Visual Appeal and style (color, contrast, fonts, white space)
    • Consistent look and feel
    • Readability
  • Design (45%)
    • Browser Compatibility
    • Mobile Friendly
  • Client Handling (5%)
    • How you satisfied the client
  • Presentation (5%)
    • Effectiveness of presentation
