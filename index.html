About Caritakathon

What is Caritakathon

Caritakathon, a portmanteau of caritas and hackathon, is a 2-day hackathon where teams of 3 design and build websites for charities and non-profit organizations. Prizes will be given to the team and charity whose website has the best execution of the client brief and fulfillment of the judges' criteria.

Participants

Each team will be comprised of a Designer, Developer and Project Manager. Each team will be randomly assigned a charity to be announced at the start of the hackathon.

Registration

Registration for the event will be done by group. Teams must submit a one-page website containing background information about each member so the organizers can determine eligibility for participation, as well as a sign of commitment from them.

TimeLine

  • AUGUST 21 Opening of signups
  • SEPTEMBER 18 Pre-event Bootcamp
  • OCTOBER 5-6 Caritakathon
