About Caritakathon

What is Caritakathon

It is a 2-day hackathon-style event on October 5-6, 2013 where ten to fifteen teams of 3 (project manager, designer, developer) will build websites on the spot for non-profit organizations or charities (the "clients"). Clients will be assigned to teams randomly on the day of the event itself.

When will it happen?

On October 5-6, 2013

Where will it happen?

This is still TBA but will definitely be within Metro Manila
